**[PageCount](https://github.com/dylanjgoode/pagecount)**

PageCount is a personal reading tracker that helps you set daily page goals, log progress across multiple books, and keep a clear view of your streaks and momentum.

### Highlights

- **Daily goal tracking** with streak visualizations.
- **Multi-book support** with a reading queue.
- **Calendar heatmap** for activity history.
- **Goodreads import** for quick library setup.
- **Weekly email digest** and optional MCP logging.
